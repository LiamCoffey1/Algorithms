Levene Speaker Series - Arlene Dickinson

Levene Speaker Series featuring Arlene Dickinson, "Dragon", CBC's Dragons' Den & Founder and General Partner, District Ventures Capital

Topic: Reflections from the Den: Growing an Entrepreneurial Firm

In this talk, one of Canada's most high profile business women distills a lifetime of experience and will share her insights on how to drive business success, with a focus on how to not only succeed but thrive in these challenging times. She'll also talk about her decision to acquire media company, The Bullet, and content development agency and digital publisher, Newsworthy Co. Inc. during a global pandemic.

Dickinson displays a palpable and infectious belief in the entrepreneurial spirit; she reminds us of the major contributions Canadians have made to international business and the entrepreneurial tradition.

The Levene Speaker Series is *free* to attend, though you are welcome to make a donation to the Levene Indigenous Pathways Program.
