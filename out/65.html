Enrolment Counselling


 

Want to discuss your options as a future U of R student? Our enrolment counsellors can discuss program options with you and help you apply! You can book an individual appointment by clicking on the available times in the calendar below.

Before you book:

  • If you are an Admitted Student, please contact the Academic Advising Office for your faculty or federated college or sign up for Course Registration Days.

  • If you are an International Student, please book a one-on-one appointment with UR International using their online booking calendar.

  • If you are interested in Graduate Studies (Masters or PhD program), please contact Graduate.Studies@uregina.ca with your questions.

  • If you are applying to Nursing or Social Work at our Saskatoon campus, you can contact Nevada.Smith@uregina.ca directly to book a date and time.

Please note: All times listed are in CST (Saskatchewan local time) and appointments are currently over the phone or through Zoom.

 

Ask a Recruiter Drop-in Sessions

Join us on Tuesdays between 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM (CST) to chat with a member of our Recruitment Team via Zoom and get answers to all your questions about attending the U of R! These sessions are for Domestic Students.

Click here to enter the drop-in session! (Zoom Meeting ID: 990 9843 7030)
