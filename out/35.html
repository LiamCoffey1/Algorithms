Welcome to the Faculty of Nursing

Nursing is a profession that is both a science and an art.  Science undergirds nursing but nursing is fundamentally relational.

You will come to know the human condition and how patients will teach you about the need for grace and dignity in living with a chronic condition.

Nurses experience joy and sorrow as they provide care to patients and their families.

All people are encouraged to take up the challenge of nursing and make a difference in people's lives.
