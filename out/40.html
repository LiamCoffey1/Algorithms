Welcome to the Executive Offices

The University of Regina’s motto –“As One Who Serves” – inspires all aspects of our work in the Office of the President. Through our collective commitment to the University's 2020-2025 strategic plan, entitled All Our Relations: kahkiyaw kiwâhkômâkaninawak, we are doing our utmost to fulfill the University's three-part academic mission of teaching, research, and service to community.

I invite you to explore our website to see how we are making the University of Regina the best possible place to live, work, study, and make a difference in the world.

Thomas Chase, PhD
President and Vice-Chancellor
