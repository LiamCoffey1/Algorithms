Kenneth Levene Graduate School of Business

 

Hill and Levene support staff are currently working remotely *BUT* they are still available to support you, our students and stakeholders, by telephone and email.

The best way to get in touch is: 306-585-6294 or Levene.Gradschool@uregina.ca

Located in the heart of Canada's prairies, Regina Saskatchewan is a premier destination for EMBA, MBA and Master Level studies. At the Levene Graduate School of Business (Levene GSB), our EMBA and suite of MBA and Master level programs are designed to help you achieve your personal and professional career goals. You can complete your degree at a full-time or part-time pace and with a variety of flexible study options. We put theory into practice using live business cases and offer the chance to learn while you earn in our enriching Co-op program. Levene GSB challenges you to think strategically. We link research, theory, practice, inquiry, and imagination to develop ethical and visionary leaders.


THE LEVENE EMBA
Experience first. Our EMBA is designed for the experienced manager who has a passion for excellence, sees the big picture, and is challenged with balancing multiple responsibilities. To ensure your continued business success, we provide the advanced tools you need to keep pace with progressive industries and modern technologies. Levene GSB has expertise working with complex organizations and competing interests. We will guide you with the insight you need to do business on a global scale.


THE LEVENE MBA
Pursue your career passion with an MBA in General Management. Our program combines cutting-edge theory with business fundamentals to give you the confidence and knowledge you need to excel in business.


OUR MASTER LEVEL PROGRAMS
Master programs specialize in leadership and people:

Master of Administration and Leadership provides you with executive skills in strategy, governance, leadership and management.

Master of Human Resource Management, MHRM is designed for experienced human resource professionals who want to maximize their competencies, knowledge and credibility.


WANT TO START SMALL?
Obtaining a Master’s Certificate in Human Resource Management, Organizational Leadership, or Project Management is an excellent way to take a few Master level courses without committing to a full Master’s program.  Master certificates can be used for credit in our full Master Programs.
