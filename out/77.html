Getting Here

Walk or Bike
The Main Campus is situated on the shores of Wascana Lake, so you can take the multi-use pathway system through Wascana Centre to get to class. Plan your route by checking the map for the pathway system on the city website.

Check the official campus map (PDF or JPG formats, above) for locations of bike racks on campus.

Transit
Regina Transit offers both regular and express bus service to the University of Regina. To learn more about these bus routes and to download a bus schedule, go to Transit Services on the City of Regina's website.

To view when the next buses are arriving on campus, go to Transit Live.com

Drive
If you drive to the U of R's main campus, you will need to pay for parking. Parking and Transportation Services' website has information about rates for meters, lot fees, daily parking permits and semester parking permits.

The locations for accessible parking, motorcycle parking, bicycle parking and visitor parking (hourly meters) can be found on the official campus map (PDF or JPG formats, above).
