    Find a Person

    Enter the name of the person you wish to find below in our searchable faculty/staff telephone directory. Results will appear in a new window.

    Search Faculty/Staff Telephone Directory

    Note: First name, last name, department, phone number, and e-mail username fields are searched. For best results type a last name or department into the field above. For a list of staff in Saskatoon, enter the city's name; for a list of staff in Prince Albert, enter Prince AND Albert.

    EXAMPLE: Art
    PRODUCES: 623 matches found for "Art"

    If you include "AND" in your search, your search will match all keywords.

    EXAMPLE: Art AND Exner
    PRODUCES: Art Exner INFORMATION SERVICES
