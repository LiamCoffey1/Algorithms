(h) Privacy

Your use of and access to the Site is subject to the University’s Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Policy.

The Site does not automatically gather any personal information from you unless you provide it to the University voluntarily through contacting us via e-mail, or through your completing and submitting an online form. Personal information (such as name, address, phone number, e-mail address) obtained from users of the Site is collected, managed, used and disclosed by the University pursuant to The Local Authority Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (Saskatchewan).

If you should choose to provide us with personal information—in an e-mail or by filling out a form and submitting it to us through the Site —we will use that information to respond to your message and to help us get the information you have requested.

(i) Information collected automatically via the Site

Information is collected on the Site through a variety of methods. While we make no attempt to link the information captured to the identity of individual visitors to the Site, logs capture aggregate, generic, and non-personally identifying information about Site visitors. The University uses a third party web analytics service to capture the following information to improve website performance and user experience:

  1. The Internet domain and IP address from which you access the Site;
  2. The type of browser and operating system used to access the Site;
  3. The date and time you access the Site;
  4. The pages you visit; and
  5. If you link to this Site from another website, the address of that website.

The University does not collect personal information for commercial marketing or distribution to any third parties.

(j) Cookies

The Site uses cookies to track how visitors use the Site. If you are concerned about the use of cookies, you can refer to the help information in your browser software for information on how to disable cookies. If you wish to delete cookies from your device, consult your browser’s help files for instructions on how to do so.

(k) Security

For the Site's security, the University employs software programs to monitor network traffic that identifies unauthorized attempts to upload or change information, or otherwise cause damage.

Except for authorized investigations, no other attempts are made to identify individual users.

(l) Access and Interference

In using the Site, you agree that you will not use any device, software or process to interfere or attempt to interfere with the proper working of the Site. You agree that you will not use any robot, spider, other automatic device, or manual process to impose an unreasonable or disproportionately large load on the University’s infrastructure. An "unreasonable or disproportionately large load" is one that prevents other members of the University community from gaining fair and equitable access to web-based systems and resources.

(m) Computer Viruses

While every effort is made to ensure that information provided on the Site does not contain computer viruses, you should take reasonable and appropriate precautions to scan for computer viruses and should ensure that you have a complete and current backup of the applicable items or information contained on your computer system. You should pay specific attention to some of the newer viruses that have been written to automatically execute when an infected word processing document is loaded into certain word processing programs.
