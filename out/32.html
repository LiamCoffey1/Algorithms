Welcome to the Faculty of Kinesiology and Health Studies

Welcome to a great place to learn and prepare for your future! Whether you are interested in studying with us at the undergraduate or graduate level, a user of our ancillary services such as Recreation, Athletics or the Dr. Paul Schwann Centre, or a valued alumnus, enjoy the journey.

 

March 30 Reminder: Current students may still register for URFit classes and reserve the Fitness & Lifestyle Centre and the Aquatics Centre! Click here for reservation information.

 

Recreational Sports & Wellness
Visit the Intramurals page for information on e-gaming, workout and activity challenges, photo challenges, geocaching, snowshoeing, the outdoor rink, and wellness programs!

Fitness & Lifestyle Centre
Now featuring brand-new Arsenal 8 squat racks from Torque Fitness, the FLC will be open for student use in 45-minute blocks. Click here for reservation information and operating hours.

URFit
Interested in spin, yoga, or workout classes? We've got in-person sessions (maximum eight registrants per class) running from Monday through Friday, and our URFit On Demand channel hosts a great deal of content you can access whenever your schedule permits! Visit the URFit page for full details.

Aquatics
We are now open for lane swimming reservations and workout classes! Check out the Aquatics page for reservation information and operating hours for lane swimming and the URFit page for detailed info on deep-water workout and shallow-water workout classes.

KHS Facility and Services Re-Open Protocols
Click here for facility access and cleaning protocols for all indoor recreation facilities and programming through the Faculty of Kinesiology & Health Studies including URFit classes, Aquatics, and the Fitness & Lifestyle Centre.

In need of clarification? Send a note to kinesiology@uregina.ca for assistance.

 

News

The Fitness and Lifestyle Centre, Aquatics Centre, and all Gymnasiums will close effective Sunday, March 15 (3:00 PM) until further notice. As well, KHS will cancel all allied health services effective Monday, March 16.
