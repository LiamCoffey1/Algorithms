Academic Calendars, Schedules and Course Catalogue

Academic Calendars

There are three academic calendars to choose from:

  • The Academic Calendar (undergraduate) contains all undergraduate program information for the current year and is searchable.  View previous undergraduate calendars.
  • The Graduate Academic Calendar is the official record of the policies of the Faculty of Graduate Studies and Research.
  • The Centre for Continuing Education Program Guide is a quick reference for programs offered by the Centre.

Academic Schedules

The Academic Schedule outlines the start and end patterns and dates associated with the delivery of instruction.
