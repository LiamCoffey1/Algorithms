COVID-19 Vaccination Clinic

The Clinic Begins March 29

This is a public COVID-19 vaccination clinic. University of Regina students, faculty, and staff will have the same access to the clinic as the public.

  • Location: Gym 2 on the east side of the Centre for Kinesiology, Health and Sport (CKHS)
  • Parking: Free parking in Lot 10 on the east side of the of CKHS
  • Dates: March 29 to July 1
  • Times: 11:30am-8pm seven days a week
  • Booking: By appointment only. Booking is based on the Province's phased roll-out by descending age groups
    • Book your vaccine online or by telephone (eligible citizens only)

BOOK YOUR APPOINTMENT

Special Vaccination Leave

Special Vaccination Leave is a government program that entitles workers to three consecutive hours to leave during work hours to receive an immunization.

Clinic Parking

There is free parking in Lot 10 on the east side of the CKHS.

Getting to the Clinic

Use this interactive map to get directions to the Vaccination Clinic on your phone.

 

Health Protocols

The clinic will operate with strict health and safety protocols in place, including; masking, sanitizing, physical distancing, limited numbers, designated entrances and exits, and one-way, foot traffic.
