University of Regina Student App

Stay connected to the University of Regina with our official app for students. The free app gives you mobile access to your class schedule, grades, important student contacts and resources.

  • Sign in using your nine-digit student ID number and PIN to access your class schedule and grades. Note: Make sure you visit the main U of R website and log into UR Self-Service to set or update your PIN, before logging into the app. If you upgrade the operating system on your mobile device, please log into the app and sign in again.

Student App Features

Check out all the features in the student app including contact numbers for safety, health and security, academic advising, and student services; a map with building photos; career services information; bus info; campus news; events; and more.

Student App Updates

Our app enables periodic updates. When a new feature or update is available, it will be pushed to your device. That means the next time you open the app, when connected to a network, you’ll receive the update automatically.

Alertus App and Emergency Notification System (ENS)

The University of Regina’s Emergency Notification System (ENS) is designed to ensure effective and timely warnings are delivered to students, faculty, staff, and visitors to campus in the event of a life threatening situation when people must take immediate action to stay safe on campus.

The ENS leverages existing infrastructure, such as computer monitors and television screens across the main and College Avenue campuses, and also pushes messaging to the U of R Alertus app, and the University’s Twitter Channel.

The ENS is not linked to the Fire Alarm System and will not activate when the fire alarm sounds. Decisions to activate the ENS are made on a situation-by-situation basis.

Find Out More About the Alertus App and ENS

Go to the U of R Emergency Info website for details about the Alertus app and the Emergency Notification System (ENS) including:

  • how to download the Alertus app to your mobile device,
  • emergency messaging on hallway notification beacons, U of R computers connected to the Novell system, and television monitors, and,
  • how to connect your personal laptop or computer to the emergency notification system.

Official Student App

The official student app for your class schedule, grades, important contacts and campus life resources. Look for the official University of Regina Student App on the iOS App Store and Google Play.

Alertus App for campus emergency notifications

Look for the Alertus Recipient app icon on Google Play or the iOS App Store. Download it to receive campus emergency notifications on your mobile device. Be sure to enable notifications for the app on your phone to receive messages.

Type in "uralerts" (case sensitive) when asked to enter your organization code.
